Meenachil River - Rain Monitoring (MRRM)

The Meenachil River Rain Monitoring initiative is a pioneering project aimed at safeguarding Kerala's water resources. By meticulously tracking rainfall and river water levels, this initiative seeks to predict future trends and facilitate scientific research. The data collected serves as a valuable resource for environmental scientists, meteorologists, and policy makers. This initiative not only aids in flood prediction and management, but also contributes to the broader understanding of Kerala's unique ecosystem.
